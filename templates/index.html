    About

  • Predictive maintenance uses machine learning to analyze data from machines.
  • It predicts when machines are about to fail.
  • This allows businesses to fix machines before they break.
  • It reduces downtime and maintenance costs.
  • Predictive maintenance is crucial today as it minimizes unexpected machinery downtime, enhancing operational efficiency.
  • It reduces maintenance costs by scheduling interventions just in time, avoiding unnecessary routine checks
  • This approach extends equipment lifespan and ensures reliability, critical in a competitive market.
  • Predictive maintenance supports sustainability by optimizing resource use and reducing waste.
